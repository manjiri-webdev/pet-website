About Our Initiative 🐕‍🦺

Pet-Friendly City is a passionate community project focused on creating safer and happier urban spaces for our furry friends. From rescue missions and adoption drives to awareness campaigns, we believe in pawsitivity!

Whether you're a pet lover, animal activist, or just someone who wants to help – there's a place for you here. Join hands with us to make every city a paradise for pets.
